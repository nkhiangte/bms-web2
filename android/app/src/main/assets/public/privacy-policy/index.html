Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Bethel Mission School ("BMS", "we", "us", or "our"), located in Champhai, Mizoram, India, operates the BMS School portal website (available at https://www.bms04.com/) and the BMS Mobile Application ("Services"). Our Services are designed to coordinate academic planning, manage student/parent portals, facilitate admissions, track grades, and streamline school communications.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our students, parents/guardians, faculty, and administrative staff through compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, utilize, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that is relevant and necessary to provide you with secure portal access, legal academic tracking, and clear institutional communications.

Student & Parent Data

  • Contact details (names, emails, active phone numbers)
  • Enrollment info, grade levels, and academic performances
  • Fee payment logs and receipt transaction verification
  • Voluntary profile pictures for student record identification
  • Hostel accommodations and residential attendance status

Online Admissions

  • Details submitted via Admission Application forms
  • Birth certificates, family status logs, academic transcripts
  • UPI configuration or scanning receipts for verifications

3. How We Use Your Information

All collected personal and academic information is strictly processed for the educational administration of Bethel Mission School. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate logins and protect user accounts (Student, Parent, and Faculty portals)
  • Report and track student grades, text performance, and exam routine listings
  • Record and monitor daily classroom or hostel attendance parameters
  • Notify parents regarding homework details, emergency alerts, or general notices
  • Assess, verify, and complete submitted online admission application processes
  • Store necessary historical student TC (Transfer Certificate) and service records

4. Data Security & Protection

Building a secure environment for student information is our utmost concern:

Durable Cloud Databases: All data assets are saved securely on Google Firebase Firestore database infrastructure protected by granular security rules to prevent any unauthorized client breaches.

Passcodes & Vaults: Portals are authenticated strictly using Google Firebase Authentication, securing login keys under rigorous cryptographical hashes.

No Commercial Sharing: Bethel Mission School does NOT sell, rent, license, trace, or share personal user details, contact logs, or student records to/with any third-party marketing companies, advertisers, or automated aggregators.

5. Account & Data Deletion Rights

In full compliance with Google Play Store Developer Programme Policies and regional data privacy legislation, students, parents, and school staff members have direct control over their stored data records.

6. Policy Modifications

We reserve the right to revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect shifts in compliance, legislative adjustments, or updates to educational portal software. Changes will be posted explicitly to this page with an updated revision timeline at the top of the document.
